### An Integrated Digital Health Ecosystem for Maternal & Child Health (MCH)

Jeevan Kosh is a unified, patient-controlled digital health platform designed to eliminate fragmented medical records in Maternal & Child Health (MCH). It creates a **single, lifelong health record** starting from pregnancy and seamlessly connects **ASHA workers, patients, doctors, AI, and IoT devices** into one continuous chain of care.

Developed as a health-tech solution focusing on rural and semi-urban India, Jeevan Kosh addresses both **first-mile (field)** and **last-mile (clinical)** healthcare gaps.
